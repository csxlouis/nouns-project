Nouns

Nouns Esports is an organization tied to the Nouns DAO, a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) that funds various community-driven initiatives. The esports branch of Nouns focuses on supporting and promoting competitive gaming across several games, including Counter-Strike 2, Super Smash Bros. Melee, and Pokémon Unite. Their activities aim to create a community-centered esports ecosystem while showcasing unique events like the Nounsvitational, a recent major Super Smash Bros. Melee tournament held in Brooklyn, NY, in December 2024

The Nounsvitational will bring together the 16 best Super Smash Bros. Melee players from around the world, serving as the ultimate "championship of championships" in an otherwise decentralized competitive scene. Winners from our previous open events throughout the year will qualify, helping establish a cohesive narrative that builds anticipation for this grand finale.

In our proposal we are requesting 3 Nouns (selected for, in our opinion, low aesthetic appeal) to be turned into 3,000,000 $NOUNS that we will distribute to our community as rewards for placements on the leaderboard. Users will be able to exchange their $NOUNS token for things such as merchandise, event tickets, or swap them into USDC using our partner Matcha.xyz

Nouns GG is partnering with the Hall of Fame Village, home to the Pro Football Hall of Fame, to host a Smash Bros. tournament with 300+ competitors. Leveraging the venue’s state-of-the-art facilities, this event will enhance its esports profile and lay the groundwork for future large-scale events as the campus grows with new attractions like a water park and hotel.
